About Us

The Heritage School, Vasant Kunj promises quality education and has the infrastructure and advanced resources to make teaching-learning process enjoyable. Our learners, parent community and the teachers are treated as one family, which believes in working in unison for the betterment of a child’s future. The school Principal, Mrs Sunita Swaraj’s commitment to education and school administration spans 19 years. Her contributions and efforts in the professional and academic growth of the students have been remarkable.
